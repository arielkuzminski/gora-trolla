Our History

How Góra Trolla Was Born

Góra Trolla was founded in 2018 by a group of musicians fascinated by sounds from centuries past. The name evokes the mountain trolls of Scandinavian legend — guardians of ancient secrets and treasures of the past.

Over several years of activity, Góra Trolla has performed at more than a hundred concerts in Poland and abroad — at early music festivals in Kraków, Warsaw, Gdańsk, Toruń, Prague, Vienna and Bruges.

Meet Us

Musicians of Góra Trolla

Ariel Kuźmiński

Bagpipes, shawms, flutes

More about Ariel

Founder and leader of Góra Trolla. A devoted enthusiast of wind instruments across all eras — from medieval shawms to bagpipes of various European traditions.

Rafał Wyszomirski

Irish bouzouki, flutes

More about Rafał

A master of plucked strings with a Celtic soul. His Irish bouzouki gives the ensemble a uniquely hypnotic quality. Also plays flutes when the mood strikes.

Krzysztof Dylewski

Mandola

More about Krzysztof

A man of four strings and infinite patience. On the mandola he conjures both the delicate tremolo of Renaissance ballads and the driving rhythms of street music.

Jędrzej Jakubowski

Davul

More about Jędrzej

The heartbeat of Góra Trolla. The davul — a great drum of Ottoman origin — turns every square into a battlefield or a ballroom in his hands. You can hear him through walls.

Maciej Wróblewski

Master of Ceremonies

More about Maciej

No concert would be the same without Maciej. As MC he warms up the crowd, leads the dances and ensures the audience — willingly or not — has the time of their life.

Jasio

One-man band

More about Jasio

A force of nature. Jasio plays whatever no one else is playing — and does it better. His musical talents defy all classification. A legend.
