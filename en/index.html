About the Ensemble

Sounds of Bygone Ages

Góra Trolla is an ensemble of artists fascinated by music from centuries past. We combine authentic instrumentation and performance techniques with a passion for discovering the forgotten beauty of early music.

Our repertoire spans troubadour songs, Gregorian chant, Renaissance polyphonic motets and Baroque music dramas. Every concert is a journey through time — told in sound.
